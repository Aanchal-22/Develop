Business Challenge

  • To predict the failure events for a long term(>5yrs) period while considering the following
  • Past Usage of the product
  • Factors influencing performance
  • Current status of the major components
  • Different failure reasons and types (fixed and variable)
  • Current hardware issues
  • Future technical upgrades
  • Future usage of the product

Solution

  • Forecasting failure rates of product through simulation techniques and estimating cost of service
  • Optimization to manage costs and failures during life of contract
  • Cash flow forecasting over the term of contract ensuring the required margin and deferred balance for business
  • Risk based pricing - Step pricing; Price with Risk sharing; Structuring payment flow across time
  • Customized Solutions - Value positioning (risk versus return) of different types of service contracts
  • Sensitivity Analysis
